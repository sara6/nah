Reporting King

Reporting King is a smartphone and tablet based app that allows your site teams to efficiently report back to the office progress on site without wasting valuable time in the office‎ away from the work. Developed over a number of years across multiple projects and thousand of reports, the reporting system is optimised for minimal training through an intuitive system designed around the end user. Once the information is submitted, the client can have the information in a variety of formats, synopsis email, detailed PDF report, online dashboard etc.
